About Our System

The System for Transparent NGO and Donation Management is a web-based platform designed to ensure transparency, accountability, and trust between donors, NGOs, and administrators. The system helps donors track how their contributions are used and allows NGOs to clearly present their work and impact.

How the System Works

  • Donor: Can register, browse NGO projects, donate funds, view donation history, receive notifications, and access impact reports.
  • NGO: Can register and get verified, create and manage projects, track donations, upload progress updates, and publish impact reports.
  • Admin: Verifies NGOs, approves projects, monitors donations, manages users, and ensures transparency across the system.

Main Features

  • Verified NGO Registration and Approval
  • Transparent Donation Tracking
  • Project Management and Monitoring
  • Impact Reports and Documentation
  • Notification and Role-based Access System

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a transparent and trustworthy donation ecosystem where every contribution is traceable, NGOs remain accountable, and donors can clearly see the real-world impact of their support.
